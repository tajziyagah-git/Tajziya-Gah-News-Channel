Our Commitment

Tajziya Gah is committed to publishing news that is accurate, balanced and supported by verifiable facts. Every report is reviewed before publication to minimize errors and maintain the confidence of our readers.

Our Verification Process

Before publishing a story, our editorial team follows a structured verification process.

  • Confirm information through official or credible sources.
  • Cross-check important facts with multiple independent sources.
  • Review official documents whenever available.
  • Verify quotations before publication.
  • Confirm dates, names, locations and statistics.
  • Conduct editorial review before publishing.

Editorial Principle

We prioritize accuracy over speed. When facts cannot be independently verified, we clearly state what is known, what remains unconfirmed and what information is still developing.

Source Evaluation

The credibility of information depends on the reliability of its source. Our journalists assess every source before relying on its information.

Preferred Sources Examples
Government Institutions Official ministries, departments and public agencies
Public Documents Court records, reports and legislation
Experts Recognized specialists and researchers
Eyewitnesses Verified first-hand accounts

Misinformation & False Claims

Tajziya Gah actively works to prevent the spread of false, misleading or manipulated information. Claims circulating on social media or unofficial platforms are independently verified before being referenced in our reporting.

Unverified rumours are never presented as confirmed facts.

Corrections & Updates

Journalism is an evolving process. If new verified information becomes available after publication, we update the story as appropriate.

  • Minor factual errors are corrected promptly.
  • Major corrections are clearly acknowledged.
  • Updated information is added transparently.
  • Readers are informed when significant changes are made.

Reader Feedback

Readers play an important role in maintaining the quality of our journalism. If you believe a story contains inaccurate information, we encourage you to contact our editorial team with supporting evidence.

Transparency

Whenever possible, Tajziya Gah identifies the origin of information used in its reporting. If anonymity is granted to a source, it is done only when there is a legitimate public interest and when disclosure could expose the source to harm.

Report an Issue

To report a factual error or provide additional verified information regarding a published article, please contact our editorial team.
