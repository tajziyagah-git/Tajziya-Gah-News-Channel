Our Mission

Tajziya Gah exists to provide reliable, timely and meaningful journalism that informs the public and encourages informed discussion. We are committed to reporting facts responsibly while presenting diverse perspectives on national, regional and international issues.

Editorial Independence

Editorial decisions at Tajziya Gah are made independently and are not influenced by advertisers, sponsors, political organisations or external parties. Our newsroom operates with complete editorial autonomy to protect the integrity of our journalism.

Editorial Commitment

Commercial partnerships, sponsored content or advertisements never determine what news is published or how it is reported.

Accuracy & Verification

Accuracy is the foundation of our journalism. Every reasonable effort is made to verify information before publication using credible and trustworthy sources.

  • Verification before publication.
  • Cross-checking important facts.
  • Review of official statements and documents.
  • Confirmation from multiple reliable sources whenever possible.
  • Prompt correction of factual errors.

Fairness & Impartiality

Tajziya Gah strives to report stories fairly, objectively and without bias. Individuals or organisations who are the subject of significant allegations are given a reasonable opportunity to respond before publication whenever practical.

Use of Sources

Our journalists rely on official records, public documents, recognised experts, eyewitness accounts and other credible sources. Anonymous sources may be used only when there is a clear public interest and when their identity must be protected for legitimate reasons.

Journalistic Ethics

We expect every member of our newsroom to uphold the highest professional standards of journalism.

  • Respect human dignity.
  • Avoid conflicts of interest.
  • Protect confidential sources.
  • Do not fabricate or manipulate information.
  • Clearly distinguish news from opinion.
  • Respect privacy unless overriding public interest exists.

Corrections Policy

If an error is identified after publication, we will review the matter promptly. Verified factual inaccuracies will be corrected as quickly as possible, and significant corrections will be clearly noted to maintain transparency with our readers.

Readers who believe an article contains inaccurate information are encouraged to contact our editorial team with supporting evidence.

Use of Artificial Intelligence

Tajziya Gah may use artificial intelligence tools to assist with tasks such as transcription, translation, research support or workflow improvements. However, editorial judgement, fact verification and publication decisions always remain the responsibility of our human editorial team.

Editorial Contact

Questions regarding our editorial standards, corrections or newsroom practices may be directed to our editorial office.
