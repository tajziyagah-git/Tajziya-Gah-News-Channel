Leadership

Qaisar Tanoli

Founder & Editor-in-Chief
Senior journalist with more than 20 years of experience in journalism, the Founder & Editor-in-Chief has built a distinguished career in investigative reporting, editorial management, and newsroom leadership. Throughout this journey, they have guided editorial teams, maintained the highest standards of journalistic integrity, and overseen the publication of impactful national and international news stories.

High-Profile Reporting & Interviews


Over the course of an accomplished career, they have conducted exclusive interviews with Presidents, Prime Ministers, cabinet ministers, military officials, diplomats, and other prominent national and international personalities. Their extensive coverage includes major political developments, breaking news events, government affairs, diplomatic engagements, and overseas assignments, delivering in-depth reporting from significant events across Pakistan and abroad

Conflict & National Affairs Expertise


Recognized for extensive field reporting, the Founder & Editor-in-Chief has covered some of Pakistan's most challenging environments, including the War on Terror, reporting from Afghanistan, Waziristan, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Kashmir, and Balochistan. Their expertise spans terrorism and security, conflict reporting, regional politics, national affairs, foreign policy, and international relations, providing audiences with reliable analysis and firsthand coverage from high-risk regions.
