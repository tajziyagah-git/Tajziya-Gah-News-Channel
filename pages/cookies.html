What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer, smartphone or other device when you visit a website. They help websites remember user preferences, improve functionality and provide a more personalized browsing experience.

Cookies do not normally contain information that directly identifies an individual, but they may be linked to information you voluntarily provide while using our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose
Essential Cookies Enable basic website functionality and security.
Performance Cookies Help us understand website performance and improve user experience.
Analytics Cookies Provide anonymous statistics about visitor behaviour.
Preference Cookies Remember language, theme and other user settings.

Why We Use Cookies

Tajziya Gah uses cookies for legitimate operational and analytical purposes.

  • Enhance website security.
  • Improve overall user experience.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow users to manage, block or delete cookies through browser settings.

  • Delete stored cookies.
  • Block future cookies.
  • Receive notifications before cookies are stored.
  • Disable third-party cookies.

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect certain website features and reduce functionality.
